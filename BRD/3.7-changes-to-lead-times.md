# 3.7. Changes to Lead Times

## 1. Sync Behaviour

Where a lead time is updated on the matrix:

### Production Lead Times

- Updates must be applied via a **scheduled sync process (daily, at night)**.
- The updated values will only be applied to **new calculations performed after the sync**.

### Warehouse Lead Times

- Updates must be applied **immediately**.
- The updated values will be used for **all new calculations from the point of change**.

---

## 2. Impact on Existing Transactions

### 1. Approved and Paid Jobcards

- Once a jobcard has reached an **approved and paid status**, its lead time must be **locked**.
- Any subsequent changes to the Lead Time Matrix must **not update or recalculate** Lead times or Due dates for these jobcards.

### 2. Orders Containing Locked Jobcards

- Where an order contains one or more jobcards that are **approved and paid**:
  - The lead times of those jobcards must **remain unchanged**.
  - Order-level lead time calculations must **continue to use the locked jobcard values**.

### 3. Unapproved or Unpaid Jobcards

- Jobcards that are **not yet approved and paid** must always use the **latest applicable lead time** based on:
  - The most recent matrix values
  - The defined sync behaviour
