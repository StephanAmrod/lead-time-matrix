# 3.6. Time Unit Handling

Lead times may be defined in:
- **Days**
- **Hours**

---

## 1. Lead Times in Days

Must be applied using **working days only**.

---

## 2. Lead Times in Hours

Where lead times are defined in hours:

1. **Calculations must be based on business hours only**

2. **Business hours must be:** 08:00 to 17:00

3. **The system must:**
   - Exclude non-working days
   - Exclude time outside business hours
   - If remaining hours exceed available hours in a day, continue calculation on the next working day
