- **Internal Lead Time**
- **Client Lead Time**

This section describes the calculations for determining the different lead times for an order, as well as a visual representation.

## 1. Determine Order Type

The system must determine whether the order is:

- **Branded** (has jobcards)
- **Unbranded** (no jobcards)

---

## 2. Branded Orders (Jobcard Aggregation)

The system must use jobcard client and internal lead times for aggregation.

### Scenario 1: Single Jobcard

- **Internal Order Lead Time** = Jobcard Total Lead Time
- **Client Order Lead Time** = Jobcard Client Lead Time

### Scenario 2: Multiple Jobcards – Same Branding Department

- **Internal Order Lead Time** = Maximum Jobcard Total Lead Time
- **Client Order Lead Time** = Maximum Jobcard Client Lead Time

### Scenario 3: Multiple Jobcards – Different Branding Departments

- **Internal Order Lead Time** = Maximum Jobcard Total Lead Time + 1 day
- **Client Order Lead Time** = Maximum Jobcard Client Lead Time + 1 day

---

## 3. Unbranded Orders

If no jobcards exist:

- **Retrieve lead time from Warehouse Lead Time Matrix** based on:
  - Order Type
  - Group
  - Quantity Break

- **Apply:**
  - Overrides
  - Adjustments

- **Apply add-ons** where applicable

- **Calculate:**
  - Internal Lead Time
  - Client Lead Time

### Important Note

For unbranded orders (including console orders), no additional client approval lead time will be added. Additional client lead time applies exclusively to branded orders' rules.
