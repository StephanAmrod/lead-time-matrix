# 3. Calculations

The Lead Time Calculation Engine must be implemented within Moyo to determine jobcard and order lead times using the configured Lead Time Matrix and defined calculation rules.

The calculation logic must be:
- Rule-driven
- Step-based
- Deterministic
- Extensible

The calculation flows provided in the supporting diagrams must be treated as the visual representation of the logic, while this section defines the authoritative rules and behaviour.

---

# 3.1. Calculation Model Overview

Lead time calculation must follow a two-level structured approach:

## 1. Jobcard Lead Time Calculation

Calculates:
- Internal Lead Time
- Client Lead Time

## 2. Order Lead Time Calculation

Calculates:
- Internal Order Lead Time
- Client Order Lead Time

---

## Consistent Calculation Pipeline

All calculations must follow a consistent pipeline:

1. **Determine matrix lead time**
2. **Apply overrides and adjustments**
3. **Determine add-on lead times**
4. **Calculate total (internal) lead time**
5. **Calculate client lead time**
6. **Aggregate to order level** (if applicable)
